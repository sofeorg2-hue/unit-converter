FAQ

Common questions

What is a unit converter?

A unit converter changes a number from one measurement unit to another, such as kilograms to pounds, meters to feet, Celsius to Fahrenheit, or GB to MB.

How accurate are conversions?

Many relationships are exact by definition, including inch, foot, pound, liter, and US gallon relationships. Approximate units are described in their definitions.

Which units are supported?

Universal Converter supports metric, imperial, US customary, scientific, engineering, digital, cooking, agriculture, astronomy, electricity, pressure, energy, and more.
