Supported units

Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin.

Common formulas

  • Fahrenheit = Celsius x 9 / 5 + 32
  • Celsius = (Fahrenheit - 32) x 5 / 9
  • Kelvin = Celsius + 273.15

Quick tip

Kelvin does not use negative values in physical temperature measurement because it starts at absolute zero.
