Length guide

What is a kilometer?

A kilometer is a metric length unit equal to exactly 1,000 meters. It is commonly used for road distance, travel, mapping, athletics, logistics, and geography.

Kilometer definition

The prefix kilo means one thousand, so one kilometer equals one thousand meters. In symbols, 1 km = 1,000 m.

Common formulas

Kilometers to meters: multiply by 1,000. Kilometers to miles: multiply by 0.621371192237. Miles to kilometers: multiply by 1.609344.
